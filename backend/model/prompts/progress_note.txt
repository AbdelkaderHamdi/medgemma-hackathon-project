Document a progress note:

DATE/TIME:
SUBJECTIVE:
- Patient's reported progress since last visit
- Current symptoms
- Response to treatment
- New concerns

OBJECTIVE:
- Current vital signs
- Physical examination findings
- Lab/imaging results since last visit
- Assessment parameters

ASSESSMENT:
- Clinical progress assessment
- Problem status (improved/worsened/unchanged)
- Complications or new issues

PLAN:
- Continue/change current treatments
- New orders or interventions
- Patient instructions
- Next follow-up appointment
